Hi!

I’m Umutesa Munyurangabo, a master’s student specializing in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Cape Town. My academic interests include computer vision, deep learning and natural language processing.

About Me

I am deeply passionate about addressing complex challenges in Africa. I believe engineering decisions should be steered towards creating meaningful and impactful solutions. As a woman, I advocate for approaches that reflect the broader social contexts women represent—this means challenging biases and grounding decisions in empathy and ethics. Engineering at its best is not only about creating effective solutions—it involves understanding the human stories behind the challenges and ensuring that solutions align with people's experiences.
