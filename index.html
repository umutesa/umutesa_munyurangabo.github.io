Hi!

I’m Umutesa Munyurangabo, a master’s student specializing in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Cape Town. My academic interests include computer vision, machine learning, natural language processing, and data analysis.

About Me

I am deeply passionate about addressing complex challenges in Africa and believe that artificial intelligence should be steered towards creating meaningful and impactful solutions. Importantly I strongly believe in amplifying women’s voices in today’s rapidly evolving AI landscape. As artificial intelligence continues to shape critical decisions in society, the need for diverse perspectives is more crucial than ever. Women bring valuable insights and lived experiences that can enhance the creation of more inclusive and effective solutions. However, the field still faces significant gender imbalances that hinder the fairness and representation in AI systems. As a woman, I strive to ensure that AI reflects the broader social contexts we represent—by challenging biases, promoting inclusivity, and addressing real-world issues with empathy.
